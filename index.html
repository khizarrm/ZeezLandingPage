About Us

Welcome to Zeez Creations! Founded in 2021 in Zambia, we've expanded to Kigali, Rwanda, specializing in handmade furniture and crafts with a focus on interior design. Our skilled artisans blend elegance and functionality in each piece, from bespoke furniture to unique home décor. We’re dedicated to transforming spaces into beautiful, personalized works of art. At Zeez Creations, we believe every space has a story, and we're here to help you tell yours. Thank you for choosing us to bring beauty and comfort into your home.
